Experience refined living in this stunning home located in the sought-after Twin Bridges community of Edmond. Boasting five bedrooms and four bathrooms across 4,200+ square feet, this property is the perfect blend of sophistication and functionality.

Step inside to discover an open layout designed for seamless living and entertaining. The gourmet kitchen serves as the centerpiece, featuring an oversized island, sleek finishes, Bosh appliances and plenty of storage including a walk-in pantry. The adjacent living area is bright and inviting, with large windows framing views of the beautifully landscaped backyard and a striking outside fireplace adding a touch of warmth.

The main-level primary suite offers a private sanctuary with a spa-like bathroom, complete with a soaking tub, walk-in shower, dual vanities, and two walk-in closets. Additional bedrooms on the main floor are generously sized, and the upstairs bonus room—with a built-in wet bar—is a versatile space perfect for relaxing or hosting guests. There is also a bath with shower and the 5th bedroom is upstairs.

The outdoor living area includes a covered patio and a cozy fireplace, making it a year-round retreat. A port-cochère driveway leads to a spacious 2-car and additional 1-car garage, ensuring both convenience and style.

Situated near top-rated schools, parks, and exclusive neighborhood amenities, this home offers the perfect combination of luxury and location.
