Buxton Team Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is important to us

Privacy Policy Effective Date: 4/2/2025
We are a local team of agents who have lived in the OKC area for over 2 decades. We know our clients personally. If we have not met yet, please feel free to visit our About Us page to learn more about us. We do offer you the opportunity to search for homes on our website for free, so that means if you would like to receive MLS listing updates, we will need to collect some information. Below tells about the information we collect.

1. Phone Number: We collect your phone number to provide you with updated listing information, messages to inform you of upcoming events and to follow-up with you regarding showings and appointments.
2. How We Use Your Information: We use your information to provide and maintain our service. Communicate with you, including sending you notifications, our monthly newsletter, and updates about the real estate market. Also to improve our service and to comply with legal obligations.
3. Information Sharing: We will not share your phone number or message content with third parties, except: With your consent, or to comply with the law, with service providers who assist us in operating our services, and those service providers are required to keep the information confidential.
4. Data Security: We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
5. Your Choices: You can opt-out of receiving messages from us at any time by replying "STOP" to a message. You can request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at 405-345-6012 or 3020 E. Britton Rd OKC, OK 73131.
6. Changes to This Policy: We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by email and by posting the new policy on our website.
7. Contact Us: If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: brent@buxtonteam.com or at 3020 E. Britton Rd OKC, OK 73131
