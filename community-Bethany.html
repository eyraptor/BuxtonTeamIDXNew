Welcome to Bethany, Oklahoma: Step into the warm embrace of Bethany, Oklahoma, a place where community spirit thrives, and history comes alive. A Glimpse into Bethany's Past: Founded with the values of education and togetherness, the town of Bethany, OK was named after the biblical place of Bethany.
