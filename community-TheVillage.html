The Village, Oklahoma

Welcome to The Village, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Oklahoma! Living in The Village means enjoying the perfect blend of suburban tranquility and urban convenience. With its rich history, vibrant community, and exceptional amenities, this charming town offers an unparalleled living experience.

The Village started as a wheat field in 1949 before a developer turned it into a neighborhood just north of Nichols Hills. In 1959 The Village officially became a city. In the years following becoming a city, The Village continued to grow in residents and size. Learn more about the rich history of The Village here: The Village
