Warr Acres, Oklahoma

Discover the Enchanting Lifestyle of Warr Acres, OK

As you step into Warr Acres, you'll be greeted by a sense of belonging that's hard to find elsewhere. The town's history whispers through its streets as the vintage architecture tells stories of days gone by. At the same time, the bustling energy of today's Warr Acres paints a picture of progress and innovation. It's a place where past and present coexist harmoniously, creating an atmosphere that's truly enchanting. The history of Warr Acres starts with developer C.B. Warr who started building in 1929 and after World War II he developed the neighborhood that started Warr Acres. Warr Acres was then incorporated in 1948. Read more about Warr Acres’ history here: Warr Acres History But this isn't just about the shops or the architecture, it's about the people who call Warr Acres home. There's a sense of cohesion and comradeship here. The neighbors become friends, and the friends become family. You'll find that the spirit of Warr Acres instantly embraces you when you explore all that Warr Acres has to offer including Route 66 and so many fun community events. So, welcome to a place where tradition dances with modernity, where history is celebrated, and where every day holds the promise of something new. Warr Acres isn't just a town; it's a lifestyle.

Embrace the Wonders of Warr Acres

Don't let the cozy ambiance fool you – Warr Acres is also a hub of modern convenience. Just a stone's throw away from the tranquil neighborhoods are urban amenities that cater to your every need. From contemporary dining establishments that serve up delectable culinary creations to trendy boutiques that keep you in style, Warr Acres effortlessly blends the timeless charm of a small town with the convenience of city living. With so many great places to eat in and around Warr Acres, you will surely have fun discovering new places. Don’t forget to go to Abel's Méxican Restaurant on NW 50th and MacArthur. They have amazing food and great service. Learn more here: Abel's Mexican Restaurant. You will also want to check-out Cajun King located at NW 63rd & MacArthur. You will fall in love with their food. But it's not just about individual experiences – it's about the sense of community that blossoms in Warr Acres. The local landmarks, eateries, and parks serve as gathering places where neighbors become friends and friends become family. With six city parks, you will have so much fun outside. Whether you visit Baker Kiwanis Park just south of the City Hall Complex with its two large pavilions with grills, swings, slides, 2 tennis courts a large grassy area for activities and a Playcraft playground system or Cherokee Hills Park which is the largest park in Warr Acres. It is nestled within the Cherokee Hills housing edition and is easily accessible. There are several play areas, picnic pavilions with grills, a large parking lot and 4 tennis courts. Playground equipment includes a colorful Playcraft system with slides, stairs and other activities for children. As you can see, there is plenty of things to do here in Warr Acres. Learn more about Warr Acres parks here: Warr Acres Parks.
